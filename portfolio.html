IT LURKS WITHIN

Year started: 2025
Status: In Development
It Lurks Within is a psychological RPG game that I am currently developing for my college thesis. Inspired by games like Sea of Stars and Slay the Princess, you follow a character named Roe, who finds themselves facing the horrors that lurk deep within a research facility, known as S.E.R.Ap.H, where they work.
